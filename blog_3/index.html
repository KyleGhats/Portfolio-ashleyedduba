Celebrating Spring Festival 2023

As the Spring Festival of 2023 approached, the Baguio City Police Office (BCPO) took proactive measures to ensure the safety and security of all those who would be in attendance. Under the leadership of City Director PCOL Francisco B. Bulwayan Jr., a total of 1,196 police officers were deployed to strategic areas within the city. These forces included 150 volunteers from the Guardians, who were instrumental in helping to monitor and secure the parade venue that was launched on September 19 at Camp Crame in Quezon City.

A briefing for the BCPO personnel was conducted by PLTCOL Domingo M. Gambican, the BCPO Deputy City Director for Operations, to ensure that all preventive measures were in place and that the safety of the public was a top priority. The goal of Baguio's Finest was to have "zero major crime" incidents during the festival, despite the influx of people. The Philippine National Police Academy (PNPA) also played a vital role in the security efforts, with various departments and groups working together to ensure a safe and successful event. As future law enforcement officers, it served as a practice to handle a huge crowd and to maintain the safety of all people. Since Baguio is one of the most peaceful areas in the Philippines, it is best to observe how they handle crimes and their people. Furthermore, by this cadets were able to socialize with other people and build a healthy relationships with the community which is very helpful in their chosen profession.

There are various hashtags used to support the said event such as #LifeIsBeautiful, #KaligtasanNyoSagotKo, #TulongTulongTayo, #MKKequalsK, and #PNPKakampiNyo. It served to remind the public that the BCPO and the PNPA were there to serve and protect the people, and ensure the safety and security of all those in attendance at the Spring Festival were of the utmost priority. It is healthy to deal with other people because of the knowledge and experience that a person can gather. Having a chance to experience these types of activities and events is a must-grab opportunity that everyone should not miss. As the PNPA Director is doing, it is now time to give importance to the education of every cadet. Cadets must experience the real situation of their chosen profession so, they can appreciate the essence of being a law enforcer.

Overall, the coordinated efforts of the BCPO, the PNPA cadets, and the various volunteer groups contributed to a safe and successful Spring Festival in 2023. The deployment of 1,196 police officers and the use of various preventive measures helped to ensure the safety and security of all those in attendance, and the goal of "zero major crime" incidents was achieved. It is a heart-warming for the cadets to have this kind of experience and achieve this kind of result.

This is a testament to the hard work and dedication of all those involved and serves as a shining example of how effective collaboration and teamwork can lead to a successful outcome. In life, there is always hope. Everything is possible as long as people work hard for it to achieve their dreams in life. Lastly, coordination and communication plays a vital role in making a good decision.
