Live as if you were die tomorrow. Learn as if you were live forever

The Philippine National Police (PNP) has launched a new program, KASIMBAYANAN, which aims to foster a synergized partnership between the PNP and the general public and religious sectors. The program, which stands for Kapulisan, Simbahan, and Pamayanan, was launched on September 19 at Camp Crame in Quezon City.Cadets of Philippine National Police Academy roved the different office sections of Baguio City Police Office, asking how they operate and perform their duties in better serving the people of Baguio. With the numerous achievements they bagged last year, the BCPO still stays to be on top rating nationwide.
