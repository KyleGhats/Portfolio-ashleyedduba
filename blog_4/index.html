LIVE AS IF YOU WERE TO DIE TOMORROW. LEARN AS IF YOU WERE LIVING FOREVER - HARVEY MACKAY

Recently, cadets of the Philippine National Police Academy roved the different office sections of the Baguio City Police Office and asked about their way of operating and performing their duties to serve the people of Baguio better. This is important because as cadets who will soon become public safety officers, it is a good move to seek advice and ask questions from officers who are already in the field. It is because, in the world of policing, nothing beats experience. Since Baguio City Police Office still stays to be on top rated offices because of the numerous achievements they got last year. Those cadets who visited their office can use and adapt the way they conduct their operations and use it when their time has come and finally serve as public officers. When you are new to something, nothing is wrong in asking questions from a senior/superior because a person needs to adjust to a certain environment. This move by the cadets of the Philippine National Police is very helpful because it will give them knowledge and an idea of what is life and how is it done in the outside world because life inside the cadet corps and life outside have significant differences.

These kinds of activities can make a big impact on improving the service of the Philippine National Police. By collecting information around different stations, the cadets can come up with an idea using all the data they gathered and create or develop a program/movement that is beneficial for the community. Visiting different police stations also boosts the community relations of the Philippine National Police Academy. Those stations will be more than happy to help graduating cadets by molding them and guiding them for they will become the ones managing a police station once they graduated and received their degree in Bachelor of Science and Public Safety. In a matter of time, these cadets will be the ones managing their own office together with its corresponding personnel and they must be educated and aware of how to do their jobs efficiently and effectively. Since Philippine National Police has different branches/offices in a single station, cadets can know their respective jobs and responsibilities so that in the future, it will be easy for them to manage their team or group.

These kinds of activities are beneficial since they allow for comparison of the variations among workplaces in various locations. In order to effectively serve their area of responsibility, they can utilize it to study or assess what an office lacks or what the office can improve. The Chief of Police or the person in charge must also keep an eye on the staff members allocated to various sections to ensure that they are carrying out their duties appropriately and maintaining the station's cycle. They will also feel happy and fulfilled if you visit them because it demonstrates that their chief/leader cares about them.
