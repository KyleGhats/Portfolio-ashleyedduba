OPlan bakal sita

In photos, on January 23,2023 PNPA Cadets and members of Baguio City Police Office, City Mobile Force Company 3rd Maneuver Platoon conducted Oplan Bakal Sita and Implementation of Curfew for Minors within the central business district, in accordance with the local ordinance and as part of BCPO crime prevention program. The purpose of these operations is to randomly check for illegal firearms and other deadly weapons that might be possessed and controlled. OPLAN BAKAL SITA is one of the best practice of BCPO- CMFC.
The police also request – and not force – the guests to open their bags, so the authorities can see if there might be a firearm or deadly weapon inside. The customer may or may not open his or her bag. Again, the customer is not forced to do anything. However, anyone who hesitates to heed the request can give the impression that contraband is being concealed.
