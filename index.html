Cadet Attachment Program

Leaders in the making

Cadet attachment program is an activity for graduating cadets of the PNPA for them to experience the real MCCOY. MASIDTALAK CLASS of 2023 were given two weeks to undergo CAP and return to their own region, cadets were distributed to almost all of the units of the PNP, especiallyin a police station. They were required to observe and learn how a police officer manages and leads his/her station and people.

“A leader is one who knows the way, goes the way, and shows the way” – Jhon C Maxwell. A great leader has the ability to inspire action through proper mentorship and uses their knowledge to educate others along the way. A leader also practices what they preach by also “going the way”.
