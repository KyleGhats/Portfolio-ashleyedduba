Reflections

Public Speaking

Mr. Ariel Tenorio

Recently, we had the privilege of listening to guest speaker Mr. Ariel Tenorio(President, of Love without Borders) speak on the topic of "Public Speaking about Being a Public Safety Officer." Mr. Tenorio's words were informative and inspiring, as he shared with us his personal experiences and insights on the importance of public speaking in the field of public safety.

Mr. Tenorio began his presentation by highlighting the crucial role that public speaking plays in the job of a public safety officer. He emphasized that, as a public safety officer, one must possess the ability to speak in a clear and concise manner, particularly when addressing the public during emergencies. The ability to convey accurate information quickly and effectively can mean the difference between life and death. He also shared that speaking persuasively is important when working on community outreach programs or trying to gain a suspect's or witness's trust. He went on to discuss the importance of being able to speak confidently and persuasively in front of a group of people, which is crucial for building trust and maintaining positive relationships with the community. Mr. Tenorio shared that, he has had to give numerous presentations and training sessions and that the ability to speak confidently and persuasively is essential to effectively convey important information and gain the support and cooperation of his audience. He also emphasized that the ability to speak persuasively is important when trying to gain the support of community leaders and decision-makers.

Furthermore, Mr. Tenorio emphasized that the art of public speaking is not only about the words you say but also about the way you present yourself. He stressed that as a public safety officer, one must maintain a professional demeanor and appearance at all times, which includes dressing appropriately, maintaining good eye contact, and using appropriate body language and gestures. By professionally presenting oneself, a public safety officer can gain the respect and trust of the community, which is crucial for building positive relationships. One of the most striking moments of Mr. Tenorio's presentation was when he shared a personal story of how he had to speak in front of a large crowd during a critical emergency. He spoke about how he had to remain calm and composed while addressing the crowd, and how his ability to speak clearly and persuasively helped ease the people's panic and fear. He shared that the experience not only tested his public speaking skills but also his leadership and decision-making abilities. The story left an indelible impression on the audience, and it highlighted the importance of public speaking in the field of public safety.

In conclusion, Mr. Ariel Tenorio's presentation was a powerful reminder of the vital role that public speaking plays in the job of a public safety officer. His personal experiences and insights highlighted the importance of being able to speak confidently and persuasively, not only in emergencies but also in community outreach programs and when trying to gain the support of community leaders and decision-makers. Furthermore, by professionally presenting oneself, a public safety officer can gain the respect and trust of the community, which is crucial for building positive relationships. Mr. Tenorio's words were informative and inspiring, and they left us with a deeper understanding of the importance of public speaking in the field of public safety.
